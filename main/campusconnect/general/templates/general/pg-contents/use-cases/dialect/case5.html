General Product Delivery Method (GPDM)

Based on user types

For registered users

Your purchased service will be mailed to your registered email account from our server. It will also be uploaded to your personal dashboard which you can access by pointing on 'My Account' at the upper right section and clicking 'My Dashboard'.

For non-registered users

Your purchased service will be mailed to your registered email account from our server. No Dashboard facilities are available since that requires a registration.
