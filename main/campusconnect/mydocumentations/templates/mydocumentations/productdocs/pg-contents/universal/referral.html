Referral Program service is created in order to help a job search candidate save time by connecting them with a recruiter.

Key features

  • In order to keep integrity of our service to the companies and recruiters we work with, we recommend ONLY those candidate profiles who have a clean history of education, and employment.
  • Referral Program service is not quickly deliverable. We need time and effort to complete review and analysis of your profile as detailed as possible. The effort takes 2-3 weeks to complete.

Use cases

  • Who are looking for job in several IT positions such as Software Enginner, Mobile Application Developer, Systems Engineer, Database Administrator and many more.

  • When a job search candidate is struggling to get interview calls from a hiring manager or technical lead.

  • When a job search candidate is frustrated becasuse his or her resume is getting rejected for almost every position they applicaed for.

  • When a job search candidate is left out of any feedback after a technical interview, and the recruiter couldnot say anything why he/she wasnot selected for the position.
