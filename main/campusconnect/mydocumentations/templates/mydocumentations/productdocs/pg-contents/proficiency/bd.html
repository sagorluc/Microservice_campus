All Services

Bandwidth services are designed to meet the needs of a job seeker when his/her employment terms and conditions are strictly regulated by work authorization permission from the USA government.

Background

Job search process for a non-immigrant is way more challenging than that of a USA Citizen for some particular reasons, such as limited Visa sponsorship opportunity from a company, Legal restrictions on standard weekly work hour under CPT visa, super strict rules and regulations for an H1B visa dependent, and so on.

Key features

  • Bandwidth helps identify the loopholes in a resume that could be critical when it is being reviewed by a Hiring Manager
  • Bandwidth helps identify the skillgaps of an applicant that could cost him/her to be selected for the next round in the job search process
  • Bandwidth helps personalized solutions to different visa category based job search candidates. For example, the resume of a candidate with OPT visa is not being reviewed by the identical way to a resume of a candidate with H1B visa. Bandwidth offers personalized services that offer solutions to the challenges imposed by different visa categories such as CPT, OPT, H1B, GC EAD.

Use cases

  • Job applicants who are college students, currently studying in universities in USA, with F1 visa and looking for a suitable job to establish their respective IT career
  • Job applicants who are graduates from universities in USA with F1 visa and looking for an a part time job and/or internship

  • Job applicants who are currently employed with H1B visa status at a company
