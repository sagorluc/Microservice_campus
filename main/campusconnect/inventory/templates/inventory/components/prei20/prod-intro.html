Pre I-20

Securing the I-20 form is a pivotal stage in the journey of candidates aiming to gain admission to universities in the United States. This document is essential for international students, as it is required for the issuance of a student visa. However, navigating through the pre-I-20 steps can be a complex process with various challenges. These challenges may include document preparation, verification, and communication with educational institutions. Recognizing the importance of overcoming these hurdles, we offer specialized services designed to support and facilitate the pre-I-20 procedures, ensuring a smoother transition for candidates pursuing their educational aspirations in the U.S. Our assistance encompasses comprehensive guidance and practical help to streamline the steps leading up to the acquisition of the crucial I-20 form.
